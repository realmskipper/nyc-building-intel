# Free Tools — SEO Traffic Strategy

Free tools are one of the highest-ROI SEO strategies: users search for the tool, land on the site, and a percentage convert to paid building reports or subscriptions. Each tool below targets a specific high-volume NYC renter search query.

---

## 1. Rent Stabilization Checker 🌟 (Priority #1)

**Why:** Massive NYC search volume. Renters constantly wonder if their apartment is rent stabilized and landlords don't always tell them. No clean, simple tool exists for this.

**How it works:**
- User enters their NYC address
- Look up via NYC Open Data — DHCR (Division of Housing and Community Renewal) publishes rent stabilized building lists
- Dataset: NYC Rent Stabilized Buildings (available on NYC Open Data)
- Return: Yes/No + number of stabilized units in the building + year data last updated

**Data source:** `https://data.cityofnewyork.us/resource/` — DHCR rent stabilized building list

**Upsell:** "Want to see this building's full violation history?" → Full building report ($6.99)

---

## 2. Landlord Portfolio Lookup 🌟 (Priority #2)

**Why:** "Who is my landlord and what other buildings do they own?" is one of the most common NYC renter questions. Bad landlords own multiple buildings with the same problems.

**How it works:**
- User enters their address or landlord name
- Cross-reference HPD registration data (landlords must register all buildings with HPD)
- Return: Owner name, all buildings they own in NYC, violation counts per building, overall portfolio score

**Data source:** HPD Building Registration — free via NYC Open Data

**Upsell:** "See full violation history for any building in their portfolio" → paid reports

---

## 3. Free Building Report (Freemium Lead Gen)

**Why:** This IS the core product but the free version drives signups. Give enough to hook, gate the detail.

**How it works:**
- User enters any NYC address
- Free: Building grade (A–F), number of open violations, last inspection date
- Paid ($6.99): Full report — complete violation timeline, complaint history, permit activity, landlord portfolio

**This is the primary conversion funnel for the paid product.**

---

## 4. Rent vs. Buy Calculator

**Why:** "Should I rent or buy in NYC?" is one of the most searched real estate queries nationally. High volume, evergreen.

**How it works:**
- Inputs: Monthly rent, home price, down payment %, mortgage rate, years planning to stay, property tax estimate
- Output: Break-even point in years, total cost comparison over 5/10/20 years, recommendation
- NYC-specific defaults (high property taxes, typical down payments, broker fees)

**Upsell:** Affiliate links to mortgage lenders, real estate attorneys

---

## 5. Affordability Calculator

**Why:** "How much rent can I afford?" — searched constantly by anyone moving to NYC.

**How it works:**
- Input: Annual gross salary (or monthly take-home)
- Output: Recommended max rent (30% rule), NYC landlord requirement (40x monthly rent rule), neighborhoods in NYC that fit the budget
- Bonus: Show average rent by neighborhood from public data

**Upsell:** Affiliate links to renters insurance (Lemonade, Jetty)

---

## 6. Broker Fee Calculator

**Why:** NYC-specific pain point. Broker fees (usually 15% of annual rent) catch renters off guard. Very searchable, very NYC.

**How it works:**
- Input: Monthly rent
- Output: Typical broker fee (15% of annual), one month fee alternative, total move-in costs (first month + last month + security + broker fee)
- Info box explaining the law (FARE Act — landlords now required to pay broker fees in many cases)

**Upsell:** "Check if your building has violations before you sign" → building report

---

## 7. Lease Break Cost Estimator

**Why:** Life happens — people need to break leases. NYC lease break rules are confusing and vary by situation.

**How it works:**
- Inputs: Monthly rent, months remaining on lease, reason for breaking (job relocation, domestic violence, military, other)
- Output: Estimated costs, legal rights by situation, landlord's duty to mitigate in NYC, recommended steps
- Disclaimer: Not legal advice, consult an attorney

**Upsell:** Affiliate links to NYC tenant rights attorneys, moving services

---

## 8. Security Deposit Calculator

**Why:** Renters often don't know the legal maximum or their rights around getting it back.

**How it works:**
- Input: Monthly rent, borough, apartment type
- Output: Maximum legal security deposit (1 month in NY), timeline for return (14 days in NYC), what landlords can/can't deduct, how to document your apartment

**Upsell:** Renters insurance affiliate (protect your deposit)

2. Landlord Portfolio Lookup

### Phase 2 — Add calculators
4. Rent vs. Buy Calculator

7. Lease Break Cost Estimator

### SEO Approach
- Each tool gets its own URL: `/tools/rent-stabilization-checker`, `/tools/landlord-lookup`, etc.
- Each page optimized for its primary search query
- Tools are shareable — social sharing buttons on results
- Email capture: "Save your results" → email signup → nurture to paid

All driving toward the $6.99 building report and $24/month subscription.
